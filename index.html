Crystal Collector

Collect Crystals to Match the Random #

Instructions:


  1. Click on Start Button
  2. A Random Number will be generated
  3. Each Crystal has a value randomly generated between 1-12
  4. Click on each Cristal to add the values to Equal the Random #
  5. Match your picks to the number YOU WIN!, Your number is less, keep playing; your number is greater than the Random Number, YOU LOOSE!
